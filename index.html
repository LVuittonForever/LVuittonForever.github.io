Biography

From a farm in Wisconsin

Adam Tomashek was born and raised in a town with three stoplights and is currently working on a double major in Biochemistry and Visual Journalism at the University of Miami. The oldest of five children, Adam had dreams of leaving his hometown and making it big since he was young. These aspirations are slowly coming true through hard work and believing in himself. While photography is a huge part of his work, his real passion is the fickle world of makeup artistry and skincare.
